Deal Or No Deal (Ganjina)

Ganjina is the Afghan version of the television gameshow Deal or No Deal. It premiered on May 30, 2010 and is broadcast on Tolo TV.
There are 20 boxes containing prizes from 1 Afghani to 1,000,000 Afghani (about US$12,800). There have been three top prize winners.
I led this show for almost three years as a producer.
